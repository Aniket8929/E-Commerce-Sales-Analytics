# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analytics using SQL

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates SQL-based business analysis on an e-commerce dataset. The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and category-wise revenue using MySQL.

The project simulates real-world business scenarios that a Data Analyst would solve using SQL.

# 📊 Business Analysis

## 1. Total Revenue

### Business Question

What is the total revenue generated by the company?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    SUM(quantity * unit_price) AS Total_Revenue
FROM order_items;
```

### Business Insight

The query calculates the company's overall revenue by multiplying the quantity sold with the unit price for each product. This KPI provides a high-level overview of business performance.

---

## 2. Total Orders

### Business Question

How many orders have been placed?

### Query

```sql
SELECT COUNT(*) AS Total_Orders
FROM orders;
```

### Business Insight

This metric measures total customer transactions and helps understand overall business activity.

---

## 3. Average Order Value (AOV)

### Business Question

What is the average amount spent per order?

### Query

```sql
SELECT AVG(order_total) AS Average_Order_Value
FROM
(
    SELECT
        order_id,
        SUM(quantity * unit_price) AS order_total
    FROM order_items
    GROUP BY order_id
) AS orders_total;
```

### Business Insight

Average Order Value helps understand customer purchasing behavior and evaluates how much customers spend per transaction.

---

## 4. Top 10 Customers

### Business Question

Who are the top 10 customers based on total spending?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    c.customer_id,
    c.name,
    SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) AS total_spent
FROM customers c
INNER JOIN orders o
    ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
INNER JOIN order_items oi
    ON o.order_id = oi.order_id
GROUP BY c.customer_id, c.name
ORDER BY total_spent DESC
LIMIT 10;
```

### Business Insight

This analysis identifies the highest-value customers who contribute the most revenue and can be targeted through loyalty programs and personalized marketing.

---

## 5. Best Selling Categories

### Business Question

Which product categories sold the highest quantity?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    c.category_name,
    SUM(oi.quantity) AS total_quantity_sold
FROM categories c
INNER JOIN products p
    ON c.category_id = p.category_id
INNER JOIN order_items oi
    ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
GROUP BY c.category_id, c.category_name
ORDER BY total_quantity_sold DESC;
```

### Business Insight

This analysis helps identify the most popular product categories, enabling better inventory planning and marketing decisions.

---

## 6. Lowest Selling Categories

### Business Question

Which product categories sold the lowest quantity?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    c.category_name,
    SUM(oi.quantity) AS total_quantity_sold
FROM categories c
INNER JOIN products p
    ON c.category_id = p.category_id
INNER JOIN order_items oi
    ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
GROUP BY c.category_id, c.category_name
ORDER BY total_quantity_sold ASC;
```

### Business Insight

Low-performing categories can be reviewed for pricing adjustments, promotional campaigns, or inventory optimization.

---

## 7. Revenue by Category

### Business Question

Which product categories generate the highest revenue?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    c.category_name,
    SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) AS total_revenue
FROM categories c
INNER JOIN products p
    ON c.category_id = p.category_id
INNER JOIN order_items oi
    ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
GROUP BY c.category_id, c.category_name
ORDER BY total_revenue DESC;
```

### Business Insight

This analysis identifies the most profitable product categories and helps management focus on high-revenue business segments.

---

## 8. Repeat Customers

### Business Question

Which customers have placed more than one order?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    c.customer_id,
    COUNT(o.order_id) AS total_orders
FROM customers c
INNER JOIN orders o
    ON c.customer_id = o.customer_id
GROUP BY c.customer_id
HAVING COUNT(o.order_id) > 1
ORDER BY total_orders DESC;
```

### Business Insight

Repeat customers indicate customer loyalty and contribute significantly to long-term business growth.

---

## 9. Product Revenue Ranking

### Business Question

Which products generate the highest revenue?

### Query

```sql
SELECT
    p.product_name,
    SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) AS revenue,
    RANK() OVER(
        ORDER BY SUM(oi.quantity * oi.unit_price) DESC
    ) AS product_rank
FROM products p
INNER JOIN order_items oi
    ON p.product_id = oi.product_id
GROUP BY p.product_id, p.product_name;
```

### Business Insight

Ranking products by revenue helps identify top-performing products and supports inventory management and sales strategy.
